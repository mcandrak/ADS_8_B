About this Plan

  • This French Country home plan gives you 4 bedrooms all on one floor and the option to build over the garage and get a 2-story home.
  • The master has a tray ceiling and his and her walk-in closets. It enjoys great privacy with the other bedrooms located across the home.
  • Step into the foyer and you'll enjoy views straight through to the back of the home.
  • The family room has a fireplace and is open to the kitchen and dining room. A pair of french doors lead to the rear porch which is deep enough to be used as an outdoor living room.
  • A study is located off the foyer and could be used as a guest bedroom in a pinch.
  • A 2-car side load garage makes grocery unloading a breeze.
